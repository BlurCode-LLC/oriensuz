To fulfill the tasks stipulated by the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated November 6, 2020 PF-6108 "On measures for the development of education and science in a new period of development of Uzbekistan",the acceptance of articles in 1 issue of the scientific journal "ORIENTAL RENAISSANCE: INNOVATIVE, EDUCATIONAL, NATURAL AND SOCIAL SCIENCES (ORIENS)" (Certificate of the Agency for the Development of Information and Mass Communications under the Administration of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 1391, oriens.uz) has been announced.

The journal accepts articles in the following areas:
  1. Exact sciences
  2. Natural sciences
  3. Technical sciences
  4. Pedagogical sciences
  5. Social and Human Sciences
  6. Philosophical sciences
  7. Philological sciences
  8. Economic sciences
